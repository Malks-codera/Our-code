Assam

Gee's golden langur

Gee's golden langur is a monkey found in a small region of Assam. It is scientificaly called Trachypithecus geei.It is endangered due to habitat loss ,poaching , and are hunted for food by other animals.

Gee's golden langurs diet consists of leaves, ripe and unripe fruits, leaf buds, flower buds, seeds, twigs, and flowers.

Conservation Status - Endangered


Spotted linsang

The Spotted Linsang scientificaly known as Prionodon pardicolor is a tree dwelling Omnivorous mammal.It can be located in Sikkim, Assam and Bengal in India other places where it can be found are Bhutan, northeastern Myanmar, northern Thailand, Laos, northern Vietnam, and western Sichuan, Yunnan Guizhou , Nepal,and southwestern Guangxi.

Conservation status - Least Concern
