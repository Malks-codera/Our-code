Red Panda

The Red Panda is a mammal species , native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China.it is scientificaly called Ailurus fulgens. The Red Pandas are legally protected in India, Bhutan, China, Nepal and Myanmar.This species of animal is endangered due to the fact of habitat loss ,degradation and much more.

The Red Pandas diet consists of bamboo, succulent grasses, fruits, insects and grubs

Conservation Status - Endangered

Clouded Leopard

The Clouded Leopard , scientificaly known as Neofelis nebulosa is a wild cat . It has a life span of around 11 years . They are endengered due to habitat distructrucion and over hunting for their skins

Like all wild cats the Clouded Leopard is a carnivores, it preys on gibbons, macaques, slow loris, small deer and wild boars .

Conservation Status - Vulnerable

Hornbills

hornbills are a family of birds found in the tropical and subtropical Asia , they are also found in Africa and Melanesia.the scientifical name for horn bills is Bucerotidae . Hornbills are threatened by hunting and habitat loss .

Most hornbills are omnivorous and eat fruits, insects, and other small animals.
